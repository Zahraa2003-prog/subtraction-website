📚 Mastering Two-Digit Subtraction

1
🔍 Let's Look at a Problem

Sometimes when we subtract, we run into a tricky situation. Let's see what happens with this problem:

52
-27
---

Look at the ones place: Can we subtract 7 from 2? 🤔

Hmm... 2 is smaller than 7! What do we do?

2
💡 The Magic Solution: Borrowing!

When we can't subtract in the ones place, we can "borrow" from our tens place!

Think of it like trading money:

🏦 1 ten-dollar bill = 10 one-dollar bills 💵💵💵💵💵💵💵💵💵💵

We can trade 1 ten for 10 ones when we need more ones!

3

🎯 Quick Tips for Success!

✅ Always check the ones place first

✅ If bottom number > top number, you need to borrow

✅ Cross out the old numbers and write the new ones

✅ Subtract ones first, then tens

✅ Check your answer by adding back!
